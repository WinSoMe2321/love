Why I Love You

Since the day you entered my life, everything changed. I found a kind of peace in your presence I never knew I was searching for. The way you talk, the way you laugh, the way you care — it all feels like home. You taught me how to love more deeply, how to be patient, how to truly connect. Even the simplest moments with you — a message, a smile, a memory — have become treasures I carry in my heart. I don’t just love you for what you do, I love you for who you are — kind, beautiful, strong, and simply magical.
